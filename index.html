ADAPT: Forecasting-Guided Diagnostic Triage for Dengue Outbreaks ADAPT：面向登革热暴发的预测引导式诊断分流框架 Jan. 2026 – Apr. 20262026.01 – 2026.04
First Author | Manuscript prepared for Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine 第一作者 | 拟投稿 Computer Methods and Programs in Biomedicine
  • Developed an adaptive diagnostic decision-support framework linking short-term positivity forecasting with cost-sensitive PCR confirmation policies. 构建自适应诊断决策支持框架，将短期阳性率预测与成本敏感型 PCR 复核策略相结合。
  • Trained an attention-guided LSTM model on 78,068 dual-verified Brazilian SINAN surveillance records from 2021–2025, achieving a held-out MSE of 0.015. 基于 2021–2025 年巴西 SINAN 数据库中 78,068 条双重验证记录训练注意力引导 LSTM 模型，在留出测试中取得 MSE = 0.015。
  • Derived an analytical decision threshold and evaluated operational robustness using Monte Carlo simulation under stochastic sensitivity and specificity assumptions. 推导解析型诊断决策阈值，并在敏感性和特异性随机波动假设下使用蒙特卡洛模拟评估策略稳健性。
  • Reduced projected false-positive diagnoses by 65.0%, improved system PPV to 0.785, and achieved a 49.4% cost saving relative to universal PCR confirmation; further assessed transferability in Zika surveillance. 相较基准策略，预计假阳性诊断减少 65.0%，系统 PPV 提升至 0.785，并相较全量 PCR 复核节省 49.4% 成本；同时在 Zika 监测场景中评估框架迁移性。
